By PixelHyperCube

Loves Me, or not?

Loves Me... ? is simple minigame from Super Mario 64 DS. It's only the only minigame in the game where a score is not displayed.

In the minigame, there's a flower in the center of the screen. By plucking of a petal, the flower will show a 'Loves Me' state first. After plucking the next petal, it will change to the 'Loves Me Not' state. This will keep on alternating until the last petal, where it will show you the final result, which ranges from:

  • Loves Me
  • Loves Me Not
  • Really Loves Me! (if 'Loves Me' was shown > 2 times)
  • Really Loves Me... Not! (if 'Loves Me Not' was shown > 2 times)
  • No State - when all the petals fall off immediately (working on it - beta âš’)

Other than that, I stumbled on one of the Youtube videos related to this that I found that it'll be interesting to code and run!

How to play:

Pluck the petals from the flower by left-clicking or grabbing (click and drag mouse) on them and see if the flower loves you or not!
